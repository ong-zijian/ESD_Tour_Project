Question: How do I sign up as a tour guide?

Answer: You may write in to help@merliontours.com

Question: Do I have to prepare anything on the day of tour?

Answer: Yes! Ensure that you are equipped with an accurate list of attendance, relevant tickets required for admission of paid facilities and most importantly, and enthusiastic mood!

Question: What if my participant does not turn up on the day of tour?

Answer: No worries! You should continue with the tour as per normal and we at Merlion will take note!
