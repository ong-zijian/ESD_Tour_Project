About Merlion Tour

Merlion Tour was founded by a team of 6 like-minded individuals who are dedicated to boost the Singapore economy as well as provide our incoming tourists with the most exclusive tours that are not like your traditional tours. Merlion Tour serves as a platform for locals to leverage on expanding their global interaction as well as provide tourists with some of the less overrated tourist attractions.
